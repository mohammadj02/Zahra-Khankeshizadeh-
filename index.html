About

Zahra

Behind the lens

A detail-oriented photographer who enjoys building quiet, cinematic moments — from family studios and newborn sessions to fashion, product and editorial.

Freelance photographer

Passionate and creative freelance photographer with experience in studio photography (family, pregnancy, baby). Skilled in fashion, advertising and portrait work, both in controlled studio environments and on location.

Comfortable directing people in front of the camera while keeping the atmosphere relaxed. Experienced in Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom, from colour grading to detailed retouching, always keeping the final images natural and timeless.

Open to collaborations, campaigns, and personal projects that centre around people, texture and light.
